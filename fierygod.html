The Fiery God Of Marrans

Bio

The Fiery God of Marrans is a three-piece rock/metal band featured ex-members of Shibo and Epilepsy Bout. It started in 2016 in Moscow, Russia. The band plays mighty modern rock with a brightly expressed metal core where alter rock and metal of the 80-90s and post metal/hardcore of the 2000s coexist.

Lineup

  • Eugeny Linnik - guitar / vocals
  • Maxim Dolgov - bass
  • Denis Nadezhdin - drums
